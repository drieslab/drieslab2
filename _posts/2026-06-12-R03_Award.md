We are excited to announce that the Dries Lab has received a new **R03 award from the National Cancer Institute (NCI)**!

This award will support us in finalizing the experimental methodology to create personalized regulatory networks from individual breast cancer biopsies — a key step toward more precise and patient-specific approaches to understanding and treating breast cancer. We are grateful for the NCI's support and look forward to sharing our progress as this work develops.
