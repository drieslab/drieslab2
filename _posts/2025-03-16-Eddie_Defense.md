Congratulations to Edward "Eddie" Ruiz for successfully defending his PhD thesis on March 16th!

Eddie's dissertation, *"Scaling Spatial Omics with Analytical Databases"*, pushes the boundaries of how we handle and analyze large-scale spatial omics datasets — work that will have a lasting impact on the field. Eddie completed his degree through the Genetics and Genomics Program at Boston University.

Eddie is the **second student to graduate from the Dries Lab**, and this defense was extra special: he was the first student to be hooded by Ruben at the graduation ceremony. A proud moment for the entire lab!
